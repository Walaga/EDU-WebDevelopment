Difference between AI,ML and DL

Artificial Intelligence(AI)-is a science that studies ways to build intelligent programs and machines that can creatively solve problems, which has always been considered a human prerogative.

Machine Learning(ML)-is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Deep Learning(DL)-is a subset of machine learning, which uses the neural networks to analyze different factors with a structure that is similar to the human neural system.
